The Situation In Ukraine

The civilians in Ukraine feel the effects of the war just as much as some of the soldiers. Over 8 million people have left the country to travel to a safe spot from the war. But the people who are still in Ukraine are suffering a lot. They are missing places to sleep, and have a short supply of food available. Many children have left their country without their parents just to get to safety from the war. Ukrainian civilians have to expect the unexpected to be able to survive. From unanounced air strikes to Russian soldier firing at multiple people in the vicinity, Ukrainian civilians can never be sure what to expect.
